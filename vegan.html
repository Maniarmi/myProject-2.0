Vegan

A vegan diet excludes all animal products, including meat, dairy, eggs, and honey, focusing on plant-based foods like fruits, vegetables, grains, legumes, nuts, and seeds. Benefits: 1. Nutrient-rich: High in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants. 2. Heart health: May lower cholesterol and reduce heart disease risk. 3. Weight management: Often lower in calories and fats, aiding in weight control. 4. Reduced risk of chronic diseases: Linked to lower rates of cancer, diabetes, and hypertension. 5. Environmental and ethical: Reduces environmental impact and avoids animal cruelty. 6. Careful planning is needed to ensure sufficient nutrients like B12 and iron.
